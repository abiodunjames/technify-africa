About The Event

Technify Africa is an online event that brings together product managers, software engineers, and engineering managers to learn and hone their skills. Technify Africa brings together IT professionals from around the world to share their stories, knowledge, and impact on our beloved continent, Africa. Technify Africa events are free and designed to help you improve your skills while also assisting startups in equipping their teams with the skills needed to scale their products.
